About Us

ENERGY SUPPLIES & SERVICES L.L.C was founded in 1987 due to a foreseen need in the local and regional market for professional kitchen equipment trading. This professionalism does not only come with best practice trading and services but with industry know-how to provide the right solution as per client’s needs. We offer unparalleled international market expertise to hospitality & laundry equipment of various USA, EUROPEAN brands. We are a supplier of all major brands of commercial kitchen, refrigeration, bakery, operating hypermarket/supermarket cold rooms, stainless steel custom fabrication & laundry equipment. Since our inception, we focused on quality products & upscale projects. Our projects divisions are fast food chains, catering companies, rigs – onshore, offshore, hospitals, hotels & complete hospitality division. We continue to strive for valued-added services while maintaining the best quality products, on time with reasonable prices in the industry with specialization on TURN KEY PROJECTS. ENERGY SUPPLIES & SERVICES L.L.C understands their requirement & suggesting the apt option, not just selling. To be the premier branded corporations we are distinctive, highly innovative, and successful in serving our customers by giving more value, whether measured by price, performance, or quality of service.
